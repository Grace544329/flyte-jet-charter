NEED HELP?

Frequently Asked
Questions

Get answers to all your questions about private jet charters, payment options,
and aircraft care with Flyte Private Jet Rentals.

How do I book a private jet?

Contact our flight specialists 24/7 to arrange your perfect journey.

What safety measures do you have?

We adhere to the highest international safety standards.

How much does it cost to rent a private jet?

Prices vary depending on aircraft type, flight duration, and additional services. We offer everything from light jets for short trips to large cabin jets for transcontinental flights.

What types of private jets are available?

Our fleet includes:

  • Light jets (4-6 passengers)
  • Midsize jets (6-8 passengers)
  • Super midsize jets (8-10 passengers)
  • Large cabin jets (10-16 passengers)
  • VIP airliners (16+ passengers)

How far in advance should I book?

While we can arrange last-minute flights (even same-day), we recommend booking at least 24-48 hours in advance for best availability and pricing.

Is catering included?

We offer customized catering options from gourmet meals to specific dietary requirements. Catering can be arranged based on your preferences.

Can I change or cancel my booking?

Our cancellation policy varies by booking. Generally, changes can be made up to a few hours before departure, subject to availability. We'll explain all terms when you book.

Can I book a multi-city itinerary?

Absolutely. We can arrange complex itineraries with multiple stops, overnight stays, or wait-and-return flights.
